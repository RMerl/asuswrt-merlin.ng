iPhone
  1. Go to Settings > Cellular, tap Set Up Personal Hotspot, and make sure that it's on.
  2. Plug your mobile phone to your router's USB port.
  3. Tap Trust button if you receive an alert that says 'Trust This Computer?' Tap Trust
Android
  1. Plug your mobile phone to your router's USB port.
  2. Go to your network setting of your Android phone.
  3. Turn on USB tethering.
